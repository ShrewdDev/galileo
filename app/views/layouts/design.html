Pricing

Galileo was designed as an organizational science tool rather than as a pure data analytics tool. By putting the organizational science (horse) before the data (cart), Galileo only uses data that - is needed, and no more. This means
- Galileo generates right-data rather than big-data
- No reliance on legacy data systems
- On-demand access anywhere and everywhere
- Fast, dynamic insights at the speed of business
Our product pricing reflects this philosophy. We use a Software-as-a-service delivery model to make our pricing affordable and recommend one of our tailored offerings for your unique situation.
Request a demo or contact us to see how our solution can meet your need!

About

Shrewd was born out of the strong desire of its founders to bring a new level of scientific rigor to organizations and people operations that relies neither simply on big-data nor simply on un-actionable organization development models, but rooted in the ‘right kind of data’ that accounts for the competencies and needs of today’s workforce as well as the fast changing realities of business.
We strongly believe that each organization is unique in its own marketplace/industry in the current time and requires a unique model that is tailored to its situation based on real data to maintain its competitive edge.
